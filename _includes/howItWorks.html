What is KOI?

We are a community of curators.

By installing the node app, you'll be contributing your device to our network. As a member, you'll be eligible to earn KOI for performing computational tasks, and you'll also contribute towards our effort to create a historical archive of the internet.

1. Install the App

KOI runs in the background, and runs computation tasks such as data mining.

2. Help the Network

Your device will gather information from the internet, and you'll get credit.

3. Save or Sell

Invest your rewards to secure the network, or use it to buy gift cards and other swag!
